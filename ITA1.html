Project Idea

Overview

The purpose of this project is to develop a small household grabbing robot, which will have a robotic arm and a chassis for moving, this robot can be used to help people with reduced mobility to pick up the things they want like food or tools, it enables people to get what they want without moving. In addition, we will try to add a voice recognition system in subsequent upgrades to make it easier for people to control it.

Motivation

The reason why I am interested in this project and want to develop it is that it can help many people with limited mobility, such as the disabled, the elderly living alone, etc., so that they have a more convenient life and improve their ability to live independently. “One billion people, or 15% of the world's population, experience some form of disability.” Nearly 200 million people have serious functional disorders, which seriously affect their lives. At the same time, the aging of the world population is becoming more and more serious. It is expected that the population over 60 will account for 22% of the world population in 2050. Therefore, after the development of this project is completed, the market demand for the product will be very high. Not only make profit, but also helps a lot of people, which better helps me realize my self-worth.

Description

First at all, the machine consists of a robotic arm and a base. The robotic arm is used to grab objects, and the base is used to move. In addition, infrared sensors, pressure sensors and cameras are necessary, which can help the robot to judge the distance and success of grasping or not. At the same time, it also makes users better remote control the robot.

The purpose of developing this product is to make it easier for people with disabilities to get what they want when there is no one to take care of them, making their lives more convenient and safer. They can stay where they are and rely on the remote control or mobile phone to control the robot and rely on the robot's camera to observe more conveniently.

Due to technical difficulties, the product will not have voice control technology at the beginning, it will only be operated with the remote control, but in order to take care of users with inconvenient hands, we will also focus on the development of this technology in the future, in this way, users only need to use language to control the machine. But this requires users to scan their home with the machine in advance and plan a route for the machine.

Tools, technologies and skills required

The open-source tools we need to use is Facebook’s PyRobot, and I also need various mechanical parts such as pan tilt, motor, wheel, circuit board, camera, sensor, etc. PyCharm is used for programming. Knowledge of many related disciplines will be used in the development process. We will face many difficulties in robot development for example, how can we set the parameters of the shrink strength so that it doesn't drop the item without damaging it? How to use the same clip to clamp different shaped items? How to set the pickup speed so as not to damage the item? And so on. which requires a lot of expertise, programming ability, mathematical statistics and certain electronic engineering skills are necessary. This requires the cooperation of people from different disciplines to develop.

Outcome

If the project is successful, we have successfully made the robot. IT’s still not a product that can be put on the market immediately. It is just an initial test machine. We will need more time to test it to ensure its safety. and functions normally. At the same time, we will also redesign its shape to make it look good and better protect its hardware. In addition, its success can also give the team more confidence and better prepare for the next upgrade.
